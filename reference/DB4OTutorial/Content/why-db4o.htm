Why db4o?

You are a smart person, right? You are solving difficult problems with software.

Often you need to persist some data while solving your problem.

However you want to focus on your problem, because that's what has value to you. Persistence is just an means to an end. Therefore persistence should be easy. You shouldn't waste your time on it:

To make persistence easy, db4o allows you to store your object graph. This means that you don't need any mapping or complex persistence code. You can keep your focus on solving your real problem.
